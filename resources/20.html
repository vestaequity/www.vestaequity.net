Top News in Housing

85% of Americans think a home is a good investment - According to a survey by TD Bank's First-Time Homebuyer Pulse, 85% of new home buyers believe that owning a home is a good long-term investment.

Why baby boomers remain competitive in the U.S. housing market - Baby boomers' ability to tap into home equity gives them an advantage over millennials in the U.S. housing market, as they can make more compelling offers on properties, leading to competition between the two generations.

Higher mortgage rates, lower social security payments: The calamity Washington wants to avoid with a debt ceiling deal - The article discusses the potential economic consequences of the U.S. not raising the debt ceiling, including possible higher mortgage rates and lower Social Security payments, with a tentative deal to raise the debt limit currently awaiting Congress' vote and President Biden's signature, while the Treasury may need to prioritize payments if the debt ceiling isn't raised by early June.
