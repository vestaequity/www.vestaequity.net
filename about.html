Our Inspiration

We created Vesta Equity to remove all the barriers in accessing property equity and create an open global marketplace for property owners and investors.

We connect investors to property owners that want to access the cash in their property to pursue their financial dreams without the encumbrances, hassles and costs of institutional loans. Our property investors are looking to add residential property investments to their portfolio without the risks, complications, and expenses of full property ownership.

1

Users undergo a rigorous KYC/AML process to protect the integrity of the solution and ensure 100% regulatory compliance.

2

All properties are vetted, professionally appraised, and ownership is verified. The property is then tokenized using Algorand’s Standard Assets (ASA) system. Full legal rights for the property owner and investor are embedded in smart contracts while investor rights are protected by Vesta Equity’s registered deed of trust instrument with the property owner and local registry office.

3

Users are provided with a blockchain wallet that operates using a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the US dollar. This ensures transactional stability for listings, offers, and closings. Users can choose to offramp funds at any point in time to a preferred fiat currency by wire transfer or cryptocurrency in minutes.

4

The platform is built on blockchain which is a distributed ledger technology that ensures a common immutable and incorruptible record of all transactions.
